How It Works

YOUR ROAD TO
THE PLATFORM

01

Apply

Fill out our intake form with your training history, current lifts, goals, and competition plans. The more detail, the better we can tailor your experience.

02

Consult

Once submitted, Liv or Jacob will reach out for a quick call to go over everything and start planning your first block. No templates — every athlete is different.

03

Train

Receive your personalized 4-week training block. Open communication and form checks are always available to help you lift your best throughout the block.

04

Evolve

Monthly check-ins review your progress and plan the next block. Meet day planning and handling included when competition day comes around.
